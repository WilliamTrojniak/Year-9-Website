Introduction

The world is in need serious need of action towards the climate. The effects of climate change are many and drastic. Sea levels rising, loss of habitats and unsuitable farming conditions, along with many others are all effects of climate change. Undoubtedly, this issue needs immediate attention, but where to start? Greenhouse gasses are a significant cause of climate change and are currently being emitted at a higher rate than ever before. However, by eliminating fossil fuelled energy sources and replacing them with clean, renewable energy sources, levels of greenhouse gas emissions have the potential to be reduced drastically, helping to slow and mitigate the effects of climate change.
